Fatalities for Black and White Victims & Weapon Status



Analysis: This graph shows how many victims of police shootings were armed with weapons specifically for White and Black Victims. The results for each weapon status level show similar patterns for Victims regardless of race (whether they were White or Black). We can see from the graph that both Black and White vicims were equally likely to be armed with a gun, or other weapons, and were equally likely to be unarmed. This is interesting since we do see racial differences in the number of fatalities when we look at percentages and rates based on population sizes.
