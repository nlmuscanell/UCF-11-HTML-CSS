Fatal Police Shootings in the United States (From 2015 to 2020)


About The Site: The topic of police officers' use-of-force and race have been prominent in the media over the last few years, rightfully so. Breonna Taylor, George Floyd, and Philando Castille (just to name a few); these names bring about many emotions. Why were these individuals killed by police officers? Is it because they were black? This website takes a brief look at this question. Are black people more likely to be shot and killed by cops? I present just a few analyses and visualizations on fatal police shootings in the United States. The analyses focus on the roles of victim race and weapon status. The data used were collected by the WashingtonPost. This is just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to using data to address this topic. There's still plenty of data to look at and analyze.
